Abroaden

Cofounder | March 2016









Overview





Duration: 2 years

Type: App Release

Role: Director of Application Development

The idea behind Abroaden is simple: connect individuals to culture. Whether seeking connection to their own culture or wanting to experience a new culture, users can travel the world locally without leaving the city.

AbroadenSeattle was released in the App Store and Google Play.
