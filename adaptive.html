Seattle Adaptive Sports


Seattle Adaptive Sports is a nonprofit, recreational organization aimed at empowering people with physical disabilities to push their beyond their while fostering a community of athletes. To fulfill this mission, the digital redesign of SAS is meant to inspire its audience under the following communication goals: unstoppable, professional, and accessible.

I want to see a more compelling digital experience around adaptive sports, so here's my take on design system for web, mobile and TV environments.
