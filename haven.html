Safe Haven


In response to the Afghan Refugee Crisis, Safe Haven is a digital volunteer board application designed for high traffic, high visibility bus shelters as a way of bringing volunteer opportunities to the community

Solution


Surveys taught us that locals have seen breaking news about the crisis in Afghanistan but feel uninformed about the subsequent refugee displacement. While our audience of young professionals do not seek out ways to help, they are open to doing so if the opportunity presents itself.

By placing touch-screen kiosks at bus stops where people are already waiting, users are able to learn more and provide support in a manner and location that is convenient to them.




















Final Design

Video




Emphasis on Convenience and Personalization


Designed to be convenient and accessible with touch-screen kiosks located in high-traffic bus shelters, users learn about opportunities to support Afghan refugees and get involved through ease and convenience

The product's "Get Involved" pathway in particular allows users to filter and discover opportunities based on their preferences, time commitment, and experience. This personalized approach to engagement may have helped to attract and retain volunteers who might have otherwise been overwhelmed by the wide range of potential opportunities.
